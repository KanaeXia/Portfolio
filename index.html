I am a graduate from Stony Brook University, who graduated with a Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science with a minor in Digital Arts. This work experience is invaluable and taught me much of what I know today about my work experience.

Being taught in many of these disciplines described above as well as in soft skills from other courses, I hope I can be a contribution to the force that drives this company’s success. Thank you for your time and consideration, I look forward to hearing back.
